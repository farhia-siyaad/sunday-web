Our commitment to the planet

Sunday uses cutting-edge plant science and simple ingredients to formulate all of our products. From lawn and garden fertilizers to pest and weed control, we design everything with the full ecosystem in mind-getting results without all the toxic stuff. We're a 1% for the Planet business, giving a portion of every sale to help preserve wildlife habitats across the country.
